Who are we?

At Meditrac, we believe that access to essential medicine should never be a guessing game. Founded with a simple but powerful vision, Meditrac was built to solve a frustration familiar to millions — arriving at a pharmacy only to find that the medication you urgently need is out of stock. Our platform connects patients and caregivers with real-time inventory data from pharmacies, giving them the ability to check medicine availability before ever leaving home.

We work closely with pharmacies and healthcare providers to maintain an accurate, up-to-date database of medicine stock across a wide network of outlets. Whether you are managing a chronic condition that requires consistent medication, picking up a prescription on behalf of a loved one, or searching for a hard-to-find drug, Meditrac puts the information you need at your fingertips. Our easy-to-use search tools let you filter by medicine name, dosage, or location — so you can find what you need quickly and plan your visit with confidence.

Beyond convenience, Meditrac is driven by a deeper commitment to improving healthcare outcomes in our communities. We understand that time lost searching for unavailable medicine can have real consequences for patients and families. That is why we continue to expand our pharmacy partnerships, enhance our technology, and listen to the people who rely on us every day. Our mission is simple: to make sure that when you need your medicine, you know exactly where to find it.
